Five billion sport participants. No verified identity. No portable record. No safe digital home that was built for them rather than built for advertising revenue.

SHOT builds all three. One platform. One identity. One clubhouse for sport at every level, from a child on a Sunday pitch to an elite academy athlete preparing for the professional game.

The model is Spotify for sport. Free for clubs and organisations. Premium for athletes, parents and individuals who want the full experience. Enterprise for federations, foundations and governing bodies.
