The Opportunity

Sport is the largest connected community on the planet. Five billion people. Every continent. Every age. And it has no infrastructure layer.

No portable identity for athletes. No safe space for young people online. No single platform connecting the club, the coach, the athlete, and the parent.

SHOT is that platform. And it has never existed before.

The Problem

A SPORT COMMUNITY
WITH NO INFRASTRUCTURE.

Athletes change clubs, change sports, change counties. No record survives. No verified identity travels with them. By the time they are twenty, there is no proof they ever played.

Coaches run development on WhatsApp, paper, and memory. Elite athletes get academy systems. Everyone else gets a notebook at best.

Young athletes access sport culture on Instagram and TikTok, platforms built for advertising. The UK Children's Code is already in force, and access is tightening.
